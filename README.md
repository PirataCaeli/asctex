# asctex



## Why the Name

The name asctex is composed of the first three letters of the words
[ASCII](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/ASCII) and the Greek word
τέχνη (art, skill, competence) or τεχνολογία (technology); therefore, it
is written ascτεχ and pronounced asctek.

The similarity, at least in name, with TeX and LaTeX comes from the fact
that the word TeX also derives from the three letters of
τεχνολογία.


## Mission

Simple, if possible like Markdown, designed for formulas, it hides the
complexity of LaTeX while maintaining its professionalism and is
oriented towards XML.

The style applied to symbols is determined by what they represent, such
as numbers, variables, constants, sets, logical statements, and so on.
